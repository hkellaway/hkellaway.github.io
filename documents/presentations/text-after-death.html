Text After Death

by Lynne Okada & Harlan Kellaway
MakeSchool // January 2015

What is it?

Text After Death allows you to schedule messages to be sent after death - and to receive messages from loved ones passed.

Homepage

→

Signup

  • Users can sign up as either a sender or a receiver
  • The same email address can be used for a sender account and a receiver account
  • The signup process differs in that senders have to generate a key-pair

Login

  • Users can login as either a sender or a receiver

Creating Messages

  • Senders can create new messages - providing message content, date, and recipients
  • The dates of messages can be either a number of days after death or a certain date of the year
  • Creating a message emails the recipients of the message with the public key telling them they've been scheduled for a Text After Death and to save the key
  • If Receivers make an account, they can also retrieve public keys there from schedule messages

Regenerating Keys as a Sender

  • Senders can regenerate key pairs from their accounts
  • Doing so will download a new private key to their system and email recipients with the updated public key

Retrieving Messages as a Receiver

  • Once a sender dies, a designated party will distribute the private key to receivers
  • Receivers can retrive messages by providing the key pair and their phone number
